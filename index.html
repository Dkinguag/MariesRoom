About

Known as a well-read music enthusiast & connoisseur amongst her peers, Claudia is no stranger to the wide range of music genres, the cultural aspects it has & the signature artists within them. Claudia draws most of her musical taste from her upbringing, personal interests, and media outlets that dive into discussions around the musical landscape. In addition to these outlets, Claudia also draws from an array of other playlists and mixes suggested by friends and family. Created in December of 2020, Marie's Room serves as a platform to house these curated playlists & personal mixes.
